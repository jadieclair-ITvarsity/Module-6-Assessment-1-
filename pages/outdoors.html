Flims Laax Falera, Switzerland

The enchanting Flims Laax Falera region in the Swiss canton of Graubünden is a dream destination all year round. In winter, snow-capped mountain peaks, picturesque villages and a first-class ski area create a fairytale atmosphere with numerous activities such as biking, climbing, hiking and more, every day turns into an adventure.

Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina

Bosnia and Herzegovina is located in the Dinaric Mountains, which makes the southeastern European state attractive for hikers and mountaineers. Thanks to the very special landscape, Bosnia and Herzegovina has served as a backdrop for numerous movies. Vast uninhabited stretches of land and wild animals such as wolves and bears can be found here.

The Ariège Pyrenees, France

The Ariège Pyrenees is an adventure destination where outdoor activities abound, with mother nature providing the perfect setting for hiking, climbing, mountain biking, cycling, canyoning, rafting, paragliding and skiing in winter. The region is home to nature reserves and a regional nature park, perfect for observing the flora and fauna that inhabit it.
